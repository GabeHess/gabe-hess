Hello, I'm Gabe Hess. I live in the mountains of Central Virginia, where I practice the Chinese martial art of kung fu, read voraciously, and write tactlessly. I also enjoy contemplating the many wonders of life and the endless stupidity of intelligent life. I believe truth to be absolute and worth pursuing, and it was with an eye towards spreading these beliefs that I started Assorted Scraps Of Wisdom And Folly in the August of 2018 and In Pursuit Of Truth in November of the same year.
